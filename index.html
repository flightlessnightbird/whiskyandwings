Whisky & Wings

Get together to eat, drink and be merry!

What's it all about?

The Whiskey and Wings club get together to eat great chicken wings and taste amazing whisky's. How it works is that each person is taksed to bring a whisky to share and some chicken wings for everyone to taste. You will find that there is such a huge amount of diversity in types of each that there is very seldom double ups on the types of wings and whiskys brought along, and it is a great way to tantalise those taste buds.

If you're looking for a fun and social evening out with great people and amazing things to eat and drink, then this is the club for you! feel free to get in touch and come along to the next whisky & wings night.

What to bring?

You are encouraged to bring a serving of wings of your making and at least one whisky to share with the rest, however if you're not sure about it just feel free to comme along and see what it's all about before commiting to bringing your own additions.
